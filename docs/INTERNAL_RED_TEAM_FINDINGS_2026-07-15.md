## Closure rule

No row closes merely because text changed. Closure requires:

1. exact schema/registry/event/reducer changes at one candidate digest;
2. positive and minimal known-bad fixtures failing at the intended layer;
3. canonicalization and reference-cycle audit;
4. full architecture validator plus bounded formal/trace suites;
5. relevant independent review lane determination; and
6. carry-forward or explicit resolution in the final candidate manifest.
