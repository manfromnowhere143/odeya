# 0000: Adopt Odeya and begin the independent foundation

- Status: Accepted
- Date: 2026-07-15
- Decider: Daniel Wahnich

## Context

Earlier append-only Inbar extraction records treated **Odeya** as a meaningful leading candidate and deferred engine construction until more research workflows had been observed. The current operator direction explicitly corrects the spelling to Odeya, identifies it as the separate frontier-research company, and asks to begin its plan and architecture now.

## Decision

Use **Odeya** as the working company and engine name and create its independent local private repository. Begin with architecture, protocol, schemas, UI/UX, evaluation, and roadmap—not speculative claims of an implemented runtime.

## Consequences

- The older candidate/defer state remains historically valid and is superseded prospectively.
- Corporate, trademark, domain, package, and jurisdiction-specific clearance remain uncompleted separate work.
- No domain acquisition, remote repository, public release, outreach, or legal filing is authorized by this decision.
