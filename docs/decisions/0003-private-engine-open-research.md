# 0003: Private engine with gated open research

- Status: Accepted
- Date: 2026-07-15

## Context

Open research can build trust, enable reproduction, attract collaborators, and contribute benchmarks. The engine, private evaluation suites, policy, operating traces, and orchestration are company assets. Research also carries safety, privacy, rights, embargo, and security constraints.

## Decision

Keep the Odeya engine private by default. Permit papers, datasets, benchmarks, and mission code to be released mission by mission through explicit evidence, rights, safety, contributor, and publication gates.

## Consequences

- “Open research” never implies every source repository or artifact is public.
- Scientific eligibility and release authority are separate.
- No license is selected for the engine during the foundation phase.
- Public projections are sealed, bounded derivatives of canonical private state.
