# Odeya Charter

Status: founding decision, 2026-07-15.

## Identity

Odeya is the chosen name for a separate frontier-research company and its private research engine. Earlier records that treated the name as a candidate or deferred engine construction are not erased; this charter supersedes them prospectively on the operator's current direction.

The name has personal meaning. That meaning does not substitute for corporate, trademark, domain, package, or jurisdiction-specific clearance, which remain separate workstreams.

The provisional web address is `odeya.danielwahnich.dev`, following the existing research-project subdomain pattern. A read-only Vercel check on 2026-07-15 found `odeya.ai` available at a quoted purchase price of USD 160 for two years; the purchase was explicitly declined. Domain acquisition is not part of the engine's critical path.

## Mission

Build a cognitive research instrument that can sustain long-horizon work on consequential real-world problems while preserving the chain from question to evidence to bounded claim.

Odeya should help strong technical teams reduce uncertainty, discover mechanisms, design discriminating experiments, and verify outcomes in domains such as frontier AI, aerospace, autonomous systems, and other high-consequence engineering. It must earn access to each domain through domain-specific evaluation and controls; the charter does not grant universal competence.

## Founding thesis

Today's research agents are useful at search, synthesis, coding, and bounded experimentation. They remain vulnerable to context loss, reward hacking, weak graders, contaminated benchmarks, infrastructure confounds, fabricated completion, and self-confirming review. The missing product is not simply a larger model or more agents. It is an evidence-native operating layer that makes long-horizon research resumable, falsifiable, inspectable, and governable.

## Investor sentence

> I'm building a separate frontier research company called Odeya, with Sentinel, Telos, and Inbar as proof that the engine can already produce serious work, and I'm raising to bring in the right people and the compute to scale it.

For precise technical materials, “the engine can already produce serious work” means the human-plus-agent research process has produced substantive proof projects whose methods are being extracted into Odeya. It does not mean the independent Odeya runtime is already implemented.

## Constitution

1. **Reality over consensus.** Agent agreement, internal scores, signatures, and polished reports are not truth. Important gates require world contact or independently recomputable evidence.
2. **Precommit before exposure.** Hypotheses, denominators, splits, metrics, falsifiers, stop rules, and claim consequences are frozen before confirmatory execution.
3. **Nulls have full weight.** A valid null receives the same attention as a supported result. Invalidity, no valid measurement, blockers, rights, infrastructure, verification, replication, transport, and revision remain distinct axes rather than alternative scientific outcomes.
4. **Every claim has lineage.** A claim must traverse to exact data, code, environment, commands, artifacts, evaluator, budget, and verifier result.
5. **Authorities stay separate.** Proposal, protocol, safety, data rights, resources/spending, execution, truth verification, external-outcome settlement, and publication cannot silently collapse into one agent or credential.
6. **Corrections are part of the product.** They are append-only, minimally scoped, linked to superseded claims, and visible wherever the old claim appeared.
7. **Unknown is not zero.** Missing, stale, unavailable, and unmeasured states remain explicit in storage and UI.
8. **Learning is grounded.** Only verified outcomes update durable operating policy. A model's confidence or review score is not a learning signal by itself.
9. **Capability is earned.** New automation follows repeated observed work and survives evaluation; aspirational infrastructure is not treated as demonstrated capability.
10. **Defensibility over spectacle.** Scientific truth governs the architecture, language, interface, and publication surface.

## Public research, private engine

The default company strategy is a private engine with selectively open research. Open work can establish credibility, invite reproduction, and create shared benchmarks. Private assets include orchestration, evaluation holdouts, policy, operating data, learning systems, and the control plane.

Release is never automatic. Each artifact needs an explicit decision covering evidence sufficiency, safety, privacy, data rights, contributor rights, security, embargo, and claim wording. “Open research” is a release policy, not a statement that every current repository is public.

## Non-goals

Odeya is not initially:

- a consumer chatbot or generic answer engine;
- a collection of simulated personalities;
- an autonomous publisher or capital allocator;
- a source of hidden chain-of-thought telemetry;
- a system with command authority over vehicles, labs, trading, or critical infrastructure;
- a production recursive self-modifier;
- a promise of general autonomous science.

## Founding success criterion

Odeya succeeds first when an independent reviewer can replay a research mission, recover its full state after interruption, reproduce or reject its central result, and understand exactly why every public claim is eligible. Research volume, paper count, model eloquence, and benchmark score are secondary.
